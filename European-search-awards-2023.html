Cardiff-based Digital Agency achieves another victory at the European Search Awards in Portugal 2023

  • 27th May 2023

D3 is thrilled to have been shortlisted in four categories at this year's European Search Awards

The event provided a fantastic opportunity to connect with talented teams and individuals from across the industry, while also exploring the captivating city of Lisbon.

We were ecstatic to win the coveted Best Use of Search (B2C) award for our remarkable work with Keto-Pro!

What are the European Search Awards?

The European Search Awards, organized by We Are Search, celebrates excellence in PPC, SEO, and Content Marketing across 44 European countries. Each year, leading search and digital agencies compete in numerous fiercely contested categories to showcase their exceptional achievements.

From optimising search strategies in retail, travel, and gaming to delivering exceptional PPC and SEO campaigns, the industry is brimming with a diverse range of outstanding accomplishments. Explore the multitude of categories that recognise these remarkable endeavors within the industry at large: 2023 shortlisted projects.

The gala dinner and awards ceremony, held at the stunning Pátio da Galé in Lisbon on Thursday, 25th May 2023, were hosted by Ricardo Pereira.

Which categories did we excel in?

D3's success over the past three years stands as a testament to our team's dedication and ambition in delivering exceptional results for our clients through data-driven approaches and cutting-edge analytics. The European Search Awards presented an unparalleled opportunity for us to gain international recognition alongside the very best digital agencies in the field.

We submitted entries in four distinct categories, focusing on driving substantial revenue growth in highly competitive markets for our client, Keto-Pro, leveraging a data-driven product strategy to enhance profitability.

Richard, the founder of Keto-Pro, established the company after successfully reversing his own Type II diabetes. He aims to share the secrets of his success and support others in adopting a keto lifestyle, driven by a deep passion for the impact of keto on health and well-being.

Faced with unique challenges, D3 collaborated closely with the Keto-Pro team, employing a data-driven approach to achieve their ambitious goals. Our objective was to elevate their Google Ads Revenue and maximise customer lifetime value. The remarkable results we achieved within a short timeframe made it an ideal showcase for our 2023 award submissions.

How did the Awards event unfold?

The awards ceremony took place at the magnificent Pátio da Galé in Lisbon on Thursday, 25th May 2023. Representing D3 was our Data & Analytics Director, Luke Maggs, who made it just in the nick of time after a delayed flight.

After everyone settled in the ballroom, Ricardo Pereira commenced the evening's proceedings, introducing the event organizers, We Are Search. We then enjoyed a delicious three-course meal while mingling with our talented table neighbors and fellow award winners from Legacy Communications.

With glasses raised, we reveled in witnessing the well-deserved recognition bestowed upon numerous talented agencies from across Europe for their remarkable achievements. Our hearts swelled with pride when Ricardo announced D3 as the winner of the Best Use of Search (B2C) Campaign.
